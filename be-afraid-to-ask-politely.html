Please Don't Be Afraid to Ask Politely

LGBT+ do understand that the correct LGBT+ terms is an ever changing minefield.

Sometimes we might appear prickly when you use the wrong term, or make an badly thought out joke.

However, despite the sensitive nature of LGBT+ terminoligy, we'd much rather you asked politely when unsure what phrase or word to use than get it wrong!

If you're unsure, simple quietly say something like "Sorry, but is the word 'Queer' okay for me to use?" or "Hey, how do you pronounce 'Zie'?"

You'll get much more appreciation for asking, than getting it wrong or awkwardly avoiding the word or phrase!
